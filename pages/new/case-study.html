A Point & Click Adventure

I am currently working on a short point-and-click RPG for kids, set in a tiny world seen through the eyes of an even tinier protagonist.

The current scope is a short playable demo to gauge interest.

It's still very early days, but I'm hoping to capture the ambience of games like Machinarium, with a hint of the tiny details of the Marauder's Map from Harry Potter's Prisoner of Azkaban!
